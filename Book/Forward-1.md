From high above, the factory bustled with a ceaseless life.

Workers swarming about the hive of machines like industrious bees bouncing between the complicated landscape that governed the structure.

The building's walls towered, and its glass facade shone, a beacon of the city's progress in an otherwise harsh and chaotic environment. However on this fateful day amidst the commotion, something sinister was stirring.

Cracks appeared on the walls, like stealthy spiders invading a beehive. They slithered, encircling the building with their ominous web, a snare for the unsuspecting. The temperature inside had dropped considerably as many of the building's commonplace utilities began breaking down, but no one seemed to mind as the ear-piercing alarms, and live footage of the engine chamber were the centre of attention.

Everyone inside the building had come to a standstill. Inside the building housed many machines for powering and advancing AI research and improvements. A few of the biggest engines powering the facility had pressure gauges which had gone far off the charts. Managers and Engineers could be seen through the monitor trying to prevent the worst, but for whatever reason, the alarms had sounded too late. When the bolts began shooting from their housings, there was no kill switch for the reactions that had begun in that chamber.

Windows of neighbouring buildings shattered, raining shards of glass upon the street below. Emergency services raced to the scene, but it was too late for many. Twenty-six who where close by to the building managed to escape, though not unscathed. The factory lay in ruins. The surrounding area was cordoned off as investigators scoured for answers. The city was left in shock and mourning, families searching for their loved ones lost in the disaster.
