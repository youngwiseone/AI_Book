Asher

Age: 40s•

Gender: Unknown (to even Asher).

Role in Story:

Rae's parental figure, a war veteran turned bio-enhancer who has become addicted to genetic modification drugs and is now a part of the government's cover-up of the true effects of bio-enhancing.

Goal:

To become the perfect version of themselves through genetic modification and drug use. To hide the truth about the dark side of bio enhancing and cover up their own addiction.

Physical Description:

Rae's parental figure is a middle-aged person with a muscular build, scars all over their body, and a menacing look in their eyes. They wear long sleeves and pants to cover up their enhanced body which have a metallic sheen to them.

Personality:

Asher is intense and driven, with a singular focus on their own survival and continued enhancement. They can be secretive and guarded, as they do not want anyone to know the extent of their modifications or drug use.

Occupation:

Asher is a part of the government's cover-up of the true effects of bio-enhancing, working to keep the addictive and dangerous nature of genetic modification drugs hidden from the public.

Habits/Mannerisms:

Asher is often seen taking pills or injections of genetic modification drugs, as they need them to keep themself alive and feeling "enhanced." They can be paranoid about being found out and will go to great lengths to keep their drug use a secret.

Background:

Asher was a war veteran who was severely injured in battle, leading him to seek out genetic modifications to keep themself alive. They became addicted to the drugs that allowed for the enhancements and has continued to take them, becoming more and more modified over time. They took Rae in as a way of repenting for their crimes and now cares for her as best they can, while also trying to hide the true extent of their enhancements from her.

Internal Conflicts:

Asher struggles with his addiction to genetic modification drugs and the fear of being found out. They also feels guilty about what he has done in the past and hopes to make up for it by caring for Rae.

External Conflicts:

Asher is a part of the government's cover-up of the true effects of bio-enhancing, which puts him at odds with those who seek to expose the dangers of the practice. They also have conflicts with Rae, who is struggling with her own feelings towards bio-enhancing and the dark side of the practice that she has witnessed.

Notes:

Asher's addiction to genetic modification drugs and the extent of his enhancements are hidden from the public, making them a tragic figure who is both a victim and a perpetrator of the dangers of bio-enhancing. The bio enhancers in the book are all trying to push bio-enhancing as the next step in human evolution and steer away from AI being the future. However as we find out, bio-enhancment is far to dangerous to be mankinds future.
