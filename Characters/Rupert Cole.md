Rupert Cole

Age: 28•

Location

Role in Story:

Supporting character, works under the government and contacts Carter about working on the investigation

Goal:

To do his job to the best of his abilities and maintain his relationship with Carter

Physical Description:

Slim build, about 5'9" with dark brown hair, often seen wearing a grey suit

Personality:

Playful, over the top, likes to tease Carter, enjoys dressing proper, has a fondness for his past working for Carter and his wife, Chel

Occupation:

Government employee

Habits/Mannerisms:

Exaggerated movements and tone, often speaks with a higher pitch, likes to tease Carter

Background:

Grew up working for Carter after he and his wife took him in. Got his current job through Chel. Hated being covered in oil and grease when he worked for Carter and now likes to dress proper.

Internal Conflicts:

Struggles to reconcile his past as a mechanic with his current job in the government, wants to do a good job but also maintain his relationship with Carter

External Conflicts:

Struggles with the tension between Carter and his dislike for AI, which is a crucial part of the investigation

Notes:

Rupert's flamboyant nature and playful teasing of Carter add a lightness to the story, which can be quite serious at times.
