"Blade" Nathan (Bio Enhancer)

Age: Seemingly Early 40s •

Location

Role in Story: Side Character

Integral for providing information to the Main Characters and the Climax of the story

Goal:

To expose the truth about the bio enhancers' control over the government while keeping himself safe.

Physical Description:

A mesh of multiple animals and plantlife merged into a humanoid shape as a result of bio enhancement, looks terrifying.

Personality:

On the surface, he appears to be a loyal and obedient Bio Enhancer to the government. However, deep down he is tormented by what he has become and the guilt of misleading Carter and Rae. He has a desire to expose the truth and reveal the horrors of bio-enhancement to the public.

Occupation:

•	Governmental official, ex-military

Habits/Mannerisms:

Has to keep using a drug to stop his body from rejecting all of the “enhancements” that have been made to him. Because of this even though the drugs don’t need to be used as much he has the habit to make sure he checks every so often that it is around for him to use.

Background:

Internal Conflicts:

He struggles with his addiction to the drug that keeps his body from rejecting the enhancements and the moral dilemma of serving the government or speaking out about the truth.

External Conflicts:

He faces opposition from the other Bio Enhancers, who will stop at nothing to keep their power and secret safe.

Notes:

Want to include a scene near the end of the book where he confronts the other bio enhancer and explains what he did as it has been weighing on his conscience. He also just wants to see the other enhancers in the room panic, as he slowly dies from not being able to take the drug anymore but taking them down with him.
