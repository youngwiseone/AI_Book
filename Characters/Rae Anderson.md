Rae Anderson

Age Late 20s •

Location

She lives in a place that is a mix of both old and new technology. On the one hand, she has seen the negative effects of bio-enhancement and is against it, so I would expect her living space to be free of any advanced technological enhancements. On the other hand, she is still a highly skilled hacker and must have access to advanced technology to perform her job, so her living space would probably have some high-tech equipment, such as a computer setup, but in a controlled and limited way. Overall, her living space would be functional and practical, reflecting her no-nonsense and straightforward personality.
