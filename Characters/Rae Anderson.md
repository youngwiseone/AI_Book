Rae Anderson

Age Late 20s •

Location

She lives in a place that is a mix of both old and new technology. On the one hand, she has seen the negative effects of bio-enhancement and is against it, so I would expect her living space to be free of any advanced technological enhancements. On the other hand, she is still a highly skilled hacker and must have access to advanced technology to perform her job, so her living space would probably have some high-tech equipment, such as a computer setup, but in a controlled and limited way. Overall, her living space would be functional and practical, reflecting her no-nonsense and straightforward personality.

Role in Story: Main Character

Goal:

To uncover the truth behind the tragedy at the beginning of the book and bring justice to those responsible.

Physical Description:

Described as a fierce looking woman with a slight scar above her lip

Personality:

Strong willed, independent, hates bio enhancers and their practices, closed off

Occupation:

Detective and Hacker

Specialist in investigations where corrupted software evidence has hidden the real cause.

Habits/Mannerisms:

Strong willed, independent, hates bio enhancers and their practices, closed off

Becomes more suspicious when a case looks too neat.

Cross-checks official software records against physical evidence and observed behaviour.

Uses LaN as both assistant and investigative instrument, often testing a system's claims against what his hybrid architecture can detect.

Background:

Her parental figure was a bio enhancer who held a lot of power in the government, but was suffering due to the enhancements. She has a hatred for enhancers as a result.

Asher's decline taught Rae the cost of uncontrolled enhancement early. She learned enough from that world to understand the mechanisms behind it, then spent years repurposing parts of that knowledge toward controlled, ethical problem-solving instead of power or status.

Rae rebuilt and modified LaN herself. Although he appears to be an ordinary assistant unit in a standard commercial shell, he is actually a hybrid of artificial intelligence systems and bio-enhancement-derived support technology. Rae keeps that hidden because most people would either fear it, covet it, or dismantle him to study it.

Internal Conflicts:

Closed off and distant, struggles with trust and forming connections due to past experiences with bio enhancers.

Struggling with trust and forming connections, haunted by the memory of her parental figure who suffered as a bio enhancer.

External Conflicts:

Investigating the disappearance of A.R.I and coming up against the powerful bio enhancer community who will stop at nothing to cover their tracks.

Notes:

Rae has a reputation for solving cases others write off as impossible by finding the point where system records and physical reality disagree.

Past successes include exposing the real cause of the Dock Nine incident after maintenance logs were falsified, tracing the Kestrel Transit poisonings through contradictions between official diagnostics and damaged hardware, and uncovering the Marrowgate disappearances by linking suspicious clinic data to concealed infrastructure nobody else thought to inspect.

Her real strength is the combination of field instinct, hacking skill, and her unusual understanding of both software systems and enhancement-adjacent biological tech.
