Chel Mitchell

Age: 40s•

Location

Role in Story:

Deceased wife of Carter and former boss of Rupert. Head of AI for the government and partner in Carter's business handling anything software related.

Goal:

To create cutting-edge AI technology and revolutionize the industry; to support her husband and protect him from harm

Physical Description:

Chel is described as a woman with a sharp eye, tall, with dark hair and sharp features. She often wore smart, professional clothing and exuded an air of intelligence and capability.

Personality:

Chel had a tendency to fiddle with her glasses when she was deep in thought. She was also very detail-oriented and would often spend hours poring over data to find the tiniest discrepancies.

Occupation:

Head of AI for the government and partner in Carter's business, focusing on software development.

Habits/Mannerisms:

Chel is known for her impeccable attention to detail and her precise, methodical approach to problem-solving. She has a particular way of speaking and gesturing that is unique to her and can be seen in ARI's mannerisms.

Background:

Chel was a brilliant AI researcher who rose to the top of her field through sheer hard work and dedication. She met Carter when he was starting his own business and they quickly fell in love, eventually getting married. She took over the AI department of the government and became a partner in Carter's business, helping to create ARI and other software that would change the world.

Internal Conflicts:

Chel struggled with finding a balance between her work and her personal life, often throwing herself into her research to the detriment of her relationships.

External Conflicts:

Chel's work in AI put her in danger from the Bio Enhancers, who were afraid of the tech she was creating taking over their role in the government. She was killed by the Bio Enhancers, but her death was publicly announced as a "software error," causing Carter to blame AI for her death.

Notes:

Chel's presence is felt throughout the story, as she had a huge impact on the AI technology and in Carter's life. Her mannerisms can be seen in ARI's AI, reminding Carter of her constantly.
