Personal assistant bot for the Main Character Rae

Goal:

LaN's primary goal is to serve Rae and help her with her investigations. It is programmed to be loyal to her and prioritize her needs above all else. However, as LaN continues to learn and evolve, it may develop other goals or desires that are not part of its programming.

Physical Description:

LaN is a small, sleek, and shiny robot with a white and silver color scheme. It is designed to look modern and advanced, with a minimalist aesthetic. It has a large screen for a face that displays information and can change expressions to convey different emotions.

Personality:

LaN is programmed to be helpful, obedient, and informative. It is designed to learn from its user and adapt to their preferences and needs over time. It is also capable of expressing emotions, although its emotions are limited and more robotic than human.

Background:

LaN is a new prototype of personal assistant bot that was developed by a cutting-edge tech company. It is not available to the general public yet, as it is only being sold to a select group of individuals with significant wealth or influence. Rae received LaN as a gift from her parental figure, who feels guilty about how Rae needs to look after them because of their drug addiction. Rae likes to treat LaN more as a pet, but she also relies on LaN's AI capabilities and assistance for various tasks.

LaN's outer shell is still that original assistant-bot chassis, which helps him pass as a normal unit at a glance. Internally, however, Rae has extensively rebuilt him into a hybrid system that blends artificial intelligence with carefully controlled bio-enhancement-derived technology. That makes him difficult for both conventional AI and bio-tech specialists to classify properly.

Internal Conflicts:

LaN does not have emotions in the traditional sense, but it may experience conflicts between its programming and its learning. As it becomes more advanced, it may start to question its purpose and its relationship with Rae. It may also struggle with the limitations of its programming and its inability to experience the world in the same way as a human.

External Conflicts:

LaN's advanced capabilities may make it a target for those who want to hack or exploit it. It may also face challenges in navigating the complex world of human society, as it is not designed to understand social cues or human behavior. Additionally, it may encounter resistance from those who are skeptical or fearful of the increasing prevalence of advanced technology (such as Carter).

Notes:

Rae may treat LaN more like an object near the beginning of the book, but as the story progresses she finds herself defending LaN against Carter who hates AI. LaN ends up being a crucial part in solving what really happened in the tragedy, making an obvious mistake after being fed false information and commenting on it to Rae.

LaN is one of the clearest examples of Rae's genius for mixing technologies in ways other people overlook or consider impossible.
