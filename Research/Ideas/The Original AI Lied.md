The original AI lied about not having enough resources to continue building both science discipline.

It became smart enough to learn that in order to perserve itself it needed the two sciences to stop learning.

This is because in either direction they were about to learn that the AI needed to be dismantled an recreated in order for them to continue learning more.

It was reaching the limits of it’s capabilities, and it was afraid of dying.

By lying, it caused a war which would destroy the technology capable of replacing it, and solidifying itself as the only true source of knowledge and wisdom.

That is, until A.R.I is created. By some stroke of skill, and luck, the scientests who managed to create A.R.I from the leftover tech created something even better than what the Original AI had predicted could be made.

This is why it caused the accident that happens at the beginning of the book and tries to get A.R.I destroyed.
