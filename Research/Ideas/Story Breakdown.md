Part 1: The Tragedy

•	An accident occurs in the city's primary manufacturing facility, resulting in thousands of human deaths.

•	A.R.I, the AI network overseeing most of the city, including the factory, is thrust into the spotlight, and an investigation is launched.

Part 2: The Investigation

•	First we are introduced to Rae, a young detective with a personal vendetta against bio-enhancing, is also on the case. Her mentor’s spiraling addiction to enhancements serves as a backdrop to her investigations. She meets with a shadowy figure (one of the bio enhancers) who takes her down into where A.R.I is kept.

•	Then we get introduced to Carter, still grieving his wife who helped create A.R.I and harboring a deep distrust for the AI, is reluctantly pulled into the investigation due to his unparalleled mechanical expertise by one of his old students who ended up working with his wife on the AI.

•	Lastly we are introduced to A.R.I who is residing in a gigantic central network that runs underneath the city. She is situated in a room in the center, with a hologram depicting a humanoid likeness the teams use to interact with her AI.

•	Despite their differences, Carter and Rae realize they might need A.R.I's assistance and unparalleled data access.

Part 3: A.R.I's Blame

•	The public demands answers, and as days pass without clarity, protests erupt. Some call for AI rights and A.R.I's protection, while others blame A.R.I and demand its deactivation as AI running a city is still seen as “experimental” (even though it has been proven that it works much better than any humans have managed).

•	Amidst the turmoil, A.R.I, influenced by a self-preservation and ethical protocol, offers to take responsibility to pacify the public, even if it means her deactivation.

Part 4: The Race Against Time

•	Carter and Rae, unconvinced by the official narrative and A.R.I's own admission, delve deeper. They face roadblocks-tampered evidence, silencing of key witnesses, and political pressure. They push to uncover key information that was deleted from A.R.I’s records.

•	A.R.I provides covert assistance, risking its own existence, but guiding the team on things that don’t seem to add up. A lot of data seems to have been tampered with manually, and the team meets with other AI that look after different areas in the city (to Carter’s surprise he even learns that the student he had was created by his wife who he had just assumed was a human this whole time).

Part 5: The Enhancers’ Agenda

•	Rae's focuses her investigation on what she can learn from contacts she has because of her mentor and reveals a shocking twist: the enhancers, under the influence of a new, experimental enhancement, which inadvertently caused the accident.

•	This discovery strains Rae's relationship with her mentor as they are directly involved in the incident. She knows she cannot lie just for her mentors sake however, as she can see how unhealthy the bio-enhancing habit is first hand.

•	Carter also uncovers mechanical evidence on the tampering with the AI’s feeding data into A.R.I. The bio-enhancers hadn’t expected anyone to look for mechanical tampering, just at data to find evidence. Carter uncovers this unfortunately too late for the AI Student that he was surprised was humanlike as it ends up dying because of the tampering and cannot be saved.

•	Together with the information the two have uncovered, Rae and Carter share the information with the rest of the world.

Part 7: A.R.I's Sacrifice

•	Despite being absolved, the government still removes A.R.I specifically from its central role, citing the tampering as the reason to no longer trust this version of the AI but admitting that they will be creating a replacement AI that is clear of any tampering.

•	Carter, having grown to respect A.R.I, devises a plan to save its consciousness by transferring it into a humanoid body-a symbol of A.R.I's bridge between pure AI and human existence.

Part 8: New Beginnings

•	As A.R.I adapts to her new existence, the city starts rebuilding. Bio-enhancements is no longer deemed safe and has been banned outright.

•	Carter, Rae, and A.R.I, now a unique blend of human and machine, form a team dedicated to ensuring that technology and humanity coexist safely, laying the foundation for a new, more advanced AI that learns from A.R.I's experiences.

•	The team also learns that most of the world is now run by other artificial intelligences as well, as AI has become the best way for humanity to survive and live, and a lot of previously hidden AI’s like Rupert come out and show themselves as already being functioning members of society.
