What part of stories I have read do I want to take for my story?

AI existing to protect humans from Aliens in Nier Automata, then finding out that Humans died out a long time ago, but protecting that fact so that the Automata can continue their fight against the AI until they are eradicated.

AI and Aliens exploring the philosophy of humans to try and derive purpose in a purposeless world from Nier Automata.

The use of a rare resource as a Power source that everyone requires to exist, such as the Spice throughout the story of Dune. Also the hint to it’s power throughout the story in showing that it can help to predict the future, but that power not being something good or bad, it just is.

The integration of nature having a sentience, that pushes back against the unnatural doings of humans to return them back to a state of Entropy instead of the Order that humanity tries to create so that it can control the world in some way, shown in Ghibli film.

Beautiful different environments that have ingenious ways of adapting and living inside of them. Dune has Arrakis a sand planet where the natives have created contraptions to retain moisture and worship water. Ghibli has very spirtual god like elements, with humans shown to have little to no understanding of the underlying reality. Nier Automata shows how AI robots have started to live in a world removed of humans, war torn but still vast and dynamic.

I enjoy reading how the simplicity of the human mind will always push to find a way to survive, no matter the environment, but the ways it adapts to do this are a mix of myth, religion, science and morals mixed together in a way a simple human mind can understand.

Too much fact is to hard to derive values. Too much morals makes decisions impossible. Too much religion leads to oppressive power. Too much science gives false promises.
