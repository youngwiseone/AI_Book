The “Magic” experienced in this world is through forgotten tech. There is a lot of tech that was created during the robot war and only during that war which was highly advanced, but not documented.

After the war, a lot of this was re-purposed into “useful” tools, vehicles, etc without really knowing entirely what the item was created for.

Because of this, a lot of it is seen as magic by the people who use it now, and there are many efforts to continue pulling the tech out and using it as it is much more advanced than anything else that can be created now, as most of the laboratories that created this tech have been destroyed during the war.
