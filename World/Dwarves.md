Robots that do manual work are called DWARVES They are short, round and stout.

They do not really think, but have been given friendly faces in order to hide some of the fact they are quite strong and can pickup several tons of weight if needed.

The robots are used for any heavy manual labour that used to be done by humans. Humans are still needed to attend to these robots, and malfunctioning dwarves are considered extremely dangerous.

These robots were originally invented during the robot world war 3 where robots where used in the lieu of men. As such, they do still have quite a large fear element associated with them, hence the reason a lot of them work underground, or hidden away in places most humans will not see them.
