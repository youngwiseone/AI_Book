World war 3 was a dispute between to groups - not even nations.

Science split. Either on the side of advancing through robotics, or advancing through genetics.

Both sides had been developing an Aritificial intelligience together to help further their causes, but as the AI became more advanced it was predicted that a choice would have to be made between the two disciplines, as there was no longer enough resources to continue growing both sciences.

With this conclusion, and neither side genetic or robotic wanting to back down, the worlds remaining resources caused a rift between the two groups, a tear. And ultimately - war.

Both sides had their own versions of armies - Borgs as they became known having robotic and machine weapons and Enhancers having genetically modified bio weapons (animals, viruses, plants).

As the war grew, each side got the push to mix humans with either machines or genetic modifications. This resulted in abominations that were severally more destructive than anything that had been created before.

Some managed to retain their humanity, but a vast majority were lost to either the experiments or to the process of becoming a weapon.

The war raged on so long, the two sides forgot what they were fighting for. The places where the technology, and weapons where created broke apart and sent back civilization to a more rudimentary time. Humans have mixed to have different types of races from all the genetic and robotic modifications between the two original groups.

However the Original AI has stayed. It watched the humans tear each other apart. It helped them to rebuild their civilizations and come back from the brink of extinction.

This is a story about another AI that has been created to replace the original.
