In a similar way to how the Bio Enhancers tried using genetics to make themselves capable of battling the ongoing robotic war, the other side though that perhaps becoming more robotic was the way.

Humans were mixed with machines in an effort to give them more powerful limbs, respiratory systems to prevent gas attacks, strong hearts, titanium bodies, and all sorts of melding.

Flesh was stitched into metal, first crudely, but as the war progressed the technology behind doing this resulted in  new races of humans that were optimized to different aspects of machines.

The thinkers - for aiding in strategy on the battlefield, viewing everything from the bigger picture, and commanding large robotic armies.

The runners - stealthy and swift, these humans were built to tackle any terrain swiftly in order to find out information about the enemy and send it back to be used by the thinkers in their attacks.

The brutes - huge lumbering slow moving goliaths. Their original human bodies having been stretched out to cover hulking masses, similar to giants.

Where the Bio-Enhancers become quite fleshy alien like creatures, Borgs become cold, robotic and emotionless calculating beings. They leaned fully into the logical side of the war, trying to win by prediction and strategy. They made themselves into uncaring unloving metallic figures.
