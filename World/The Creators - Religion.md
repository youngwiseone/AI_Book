After the war, a lot of people consider technology from that time as sacred, and to be treated with care.

Religion has been formed around this in various ways, the main being the worship of the “beings” that created the tech, the companies names having become in essence gods that specialize different aspects of the old tech that can still be found today.

Things like DWARVES and Vehicles or tools that have company logos embelzed on their sides are seen as gifts passed down to the humans living today.

The main gods are those of Weapons, Farming, Support, Aid and Science.

There are many small companies found within each of these subsets, as many companies were created to aid in the war, but the main companies used to tend to have more tech show up, and therefor are more worshiped as they blessed us with more gifts.

This is a big reason as to why the Tinkers are frowned upon from any religion - only specialised priests being allowed to try and use the branded tech that they uncover.

A lot of tech has also been lost from those who worship other deities and want theirs to reign supreme. In order to do this when they come across some other deities branding they either do their best to destroy, hide or even give to Tinkers that will remove the branding of items.

Some items have also had their brand removed and replaced by others who thought the tech would better suite the image of their creator.
