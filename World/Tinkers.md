A tinker is a person who uses old war time tech and integrated it into current tech to make something new and otherwise unobtainable with the current days understanding of technology.

These people do not have a high status in society as a lot of people are superstitious about using old technology thinking it more to be “cursed”.

Because of this, Tinkers normally find themselves either working in small communities, or for war time efforts by the government to create defenses that otherwise would not be available.
