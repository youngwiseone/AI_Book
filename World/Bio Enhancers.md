Bio Enhancers are people who were modified genetically during the war in order to become as strong as the robots that primarily made up the armies of each warring nation.

This caused many different “races” of people to be created, each suited to different millitary operations.

There are also Hyper Enhancers who went beyond just one specific use and combined multiple genetic modifications. Generally these are people who got addicted to “upgrading” themselves.

Because of this addictive tendency in Enhancers, there was an outlaw on any future genetic manipulation on humans as it was deemed inhumane.

The prodominate cause for this is from the drugs that are needed to keep the person alive themselves being quite addictive to take, similar to heroin. Bio-Enhancers need the drug in small amounts in order for their bodies to not recognize the modified parts as “other” and try to remove them.

Because of this, the drugs have the side affect of preventing the body from identifying actually harmful cells, which lead to a lot of enhancers dying from cancer and other bodily type deaths, which can become quite grotesque and horrific.

The drug itself becomes an allievant to the pain caused by this, hence why it becomes quite addictive. But as there are set amounts you can be given depending on your level of enhancement, a lot opted for pushing more enhancement in order to get more relief.

Because of this death cycle cause in the nobel warriors who fought, a lot of this was hidden from the public eye, and outlawed.

There have been some enhancers who seem to have managed stability through different genetics mixing together which allows them to regenerate before they die. They have passed the point of ever looking human again though.

By merging themselves and their genetics with various different types of animals, these humans took more the form of beast, becoming animistic in nature, and ravenous.
